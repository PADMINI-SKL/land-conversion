Waiver of Conversion fine is available only to Manufacturing MSMEs and large,mega,ultra mega and super mega and Selectedd Service Enterprises listed at Annexure-1, The waiver of conversion fine will be reimbrsed to the eligible enterprises after implementation of projects i.e. after commencement of the commercial production by the enterprises.

Procedure for claiming land conversion fine reimbursement

The eligible Micro, Small, Medium, Large mega, ultra mega and super mega enterprises, shall applu to the respective Joint Director, District Industries Centers along with the following documents for claiming reimbursement of conversion fine;

  1. Aplication in plain papper/letterhead.
  2. Copy of the IEM acknowlwdgement part II or industrial license.
  3. Copy of approved building plan and building construction license.
  4. Copy of Land documents/records.
  5. Copy of land conversion order issued by Revenue Department and Copy of reciept/challan as proof of payment made towards land conversion charges.
  6. Copy of Project Report, duly signed by the promoter.
  7. Copy of Single Window Clearance Committee approvals/Govt order if any.
  8. Copy of ESCOMs power sanction and service letter.
  9. Copy of First Sale Invoince in respect of individual enterprises.
  10. Copy of Layout map showing the land utilisation in respect to individual enterprise.
  11. Copy of Partnership dead/ memprandum and Articles of Association /Bye-laws.

Scrutiny of the claims received for waiver of land conversion fine reimbursement.

The Joint Director, District Industries Centre after verification of the eligibility of the MSME's and also large projects less than 50 crores investments the proposala has to be placed before the DLC for sanctions and issue the sanction order in the prescribed formal as in Axnnexure-18 and maintain seperate seniority list of such sanctions.
